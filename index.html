What makes it different

Built for people who want to understand their retirement

Most tools tell you a number. Four Buckets shows you the math behind it — and lets you change every assumption.

📊

Four-Bucket Tracking

Tax-Deferred, Tax-Free (Roth), Taxable, and HSA — tracked separately because they grow, get taxed, and get withdrawn differently. Your net worth is not just one number.

📋

Real RMD Calculations

Required Minimum Distributions calculated year by year from the IRS Uniform Lifetime Table — including the SECURE 2.0 change to age 75 for those born 1960+. No estimating.

🎲

Monte Carlo Simulation

1,000 randomized scenarios test your plan against market volatility. See the probability your money lasts, not just the optimistic straight-line forecast.

🏛️

Social Security Optimizer

Compare claiming at 62 vs. 67 vs. 70. See the crossover age and lifetime total for each option. The right answer depends on your specific situation.

🏠

Mortgage Integration

Your mortgage is part of your retirement plan. Track payoff age, interest cost, and how your payment affects the portfolio draw year by year.

⚙️

Every Assumption is Yours

Pre-retirement return, in-retirement return, spending goal, SS claiming age — all configurable. No black-box assumptions you can't see or change.
